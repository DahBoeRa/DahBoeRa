The American Dream, a fragile guise

A symbol both cherished and resented

Doomed since its creation and etched in stone

Raised by conquest and holy dominion

Built on the backs of restless dreamers

Generationally, desperately passed down.

Shadows linger, stories left unsaid

Bound in silence, yet crying out

Broken masks held firm on faces worn

The weight of a promise, thinly veiled

Self fractured, yet somehow whole

Anonymous, yet coded in the cloud. Where truths find voice.
